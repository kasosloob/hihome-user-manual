### Change Password

---

User can change the password of his/her account by the following steps:

### Website

<h4><strong>Step (1)</strong></h4>
Navigate to the user avatar on the navigation bar and click the change password. (01-host-experience).

<img height="200px" width="950px" src="/images/experiences/host/01-host-experience.png"></img>

<h4><strong>Step (2)</strong></h4>
User is required is the enter Old Password and new password twice as shown on below image (01-edit)

<img height="400px" width="450px" src="/images/edit/01-edit.png"></img>

---

### Mobile

<h4><strong>Step (1)</strong></h4>
Click on the profile Icon at the bottom right of the screen.

<img height="700px" width="450px" src="/images/edit/03-edit.jpg"></img>

<h4><strong>Step (2)</strong></h4>
Click on Profile on the right side.

<img height="700px" width="450px" src="/images/edit/04-edit.jpg"></img>

<h4><strong>Step (3)</strong></h4>
Scrol down and click on edit profile.

<img height="700px" width="450px" src="/images/edit/05-edit.jpg"></img>

<h4><strong>Step (4)</strong></h4>
Make the desired changes and click confirm password.

<img height="700px" width="450px" src="/images/edit/06-edit.jpg"></img>
